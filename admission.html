English Language Profficiency

Students whose native language is not English must submit evidence of Proficiency in the language. Students must meet the minimum English standard as stipulated below to commence their registered Advanced Diploma courses with Raffles Design Institute.


Level of English Profficiency

- TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language) with minimum score of 500 (paper-based) or 59 to 60 (internet-based), taken during the last two years

- IELTS (International English Language Testing System) with a minimum score of 5.0, taken during the last two years


Test Date

Students who do not meet the maximum requirement as stipulated above will need to take the Raffles Design Institute English Placement Test
