    Raffles Design Institute is a design school based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, It is an institute emerging from the partnership between Raffles International Institutes and Tamkeen Company, that offers internationally accredited advanced diplomas. Our curriculum is designed to empower Saudi female students to innovate, and we offer specialized design courses that aim to help them improve their creative skills and prepare them with all the knowledge they need to enter the job market, putting them on the path to success.

About Our University

Raffles Education Institute, established in 2014, is one of a chain of thirty branches of Raffles Institutes which was established in 1990 in Singapore, and one of the first private institutions to offer courses in Fashion Design.

Within 5 years, multiple majors were offered at Raffles University, including (graphic design - product design - jewelry design - interior design - game design - animation design).

In 2004, Raffles Design Institute was awarded the Singapore Quality Readiness for Special Education Organizations. In 2005, Raffles was awarded the CaseTrust Award for Education.
In 2008, Raffles Design Institute was recognized by Edupoll as one of the top ten educational institutions in Singapore and the largest provider of private education globally.

A Vibrant Campus

The campus of Raffles Design Institute is located on Al-Orouba Street on the corner of Al-Amir Turki Bin Abdulaziz Al Awal Road. The campus of Raffles Design Institute is a vibrant independent campus as it is located near many facilities such as: (a hotel with a restaurant lounge – spa – gallery – restaurant),

and it is also located near the world-famous design and shopping center in Al-Olaya. The campus of Raffles Design Institute provides a safe and comfortable environment for learning and is equipped with high levels of security, because the safety and comfort of our students is our priority.
